About Me

I graduated from St. John's University in Minnesota in 2018, majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Math. In the Fall of 2018 I began my PhD in Computer Science at the University of Oregon advised by Dr. Dejing Dou and Dr. Daniel Lowd. I am currently researching techniques to make deep neural networks more interpretable. My hope is to leverage the knowledge I gain to help build provably safe and beneficial machine learning systems.
